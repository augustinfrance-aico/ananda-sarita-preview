Meet Ma Ananda Sarita

A disciple of Osho.
A master of the living path.

For over thirty years, Ma Ananda Sarita has dedicated her life to awakening the sacred essence in men, women, and couples across the globe.

Her teachings blend the ancient wisdom of classical Tantra with the living presence of a master who walks the path daily. Through retreats, trainings, and transmissions in more than forty countries, her work has touched tens of thousands of lives.
